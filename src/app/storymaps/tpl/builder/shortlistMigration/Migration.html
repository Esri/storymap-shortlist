Welcome to the Shortlist Builder

Your web map contains point data. Do you want to import those points into your Shortlist as places?
You will be able to edit, manage and add to your places in the Shorlist Builder. A copy of your web map is automatically created so your original data is not modified.
Your points will be displayed on your Shortlist map but won't be used as places. Instead you'll add your places into your Shortlist in the Builder.
